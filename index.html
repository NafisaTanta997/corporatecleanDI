CORPORATE CLEAN

Providing consulting services to help firms in NYC fulfill their corporate social responsibility.

Our Mission

The problem of greenhouse gas emissions on the environment is continually increasing. Our environment is suffering because of our habits, and as a result global warming is increasing. It is of utmost importance that we start making eco-friendly practices a habit, especially within businesses.

To provide consulting services to companies who want to take on corporate social responsibility. With strategic plans to take action against climate change by using alternative and efficient methods to run an eco-friendly business. Corporate Clean’s objective is to assist our clients in the transition to an eco-friendly space that benefits the company and consumers. Through the use of our research and expertise, we will help companies participate in the fight against climate change.

Our Services

Drawing on our consulting, effective planning, and reports, we can propose solutions and implement the change our clients need to accomplish an eco-friendly environment.

Bi-weekly Meetings

Our consultants would meet with you bi-weekly at the office our services to help consultants get an idea of what changes are critical to help you transition to an eco-friendly environment.

Effective Planning

Based on the expenses of the company and the blueprint of the building, we create a plan to reduce energy waste and reduce company expenses with what is provided based in the bi-weekly meetings.

Monthly Reports

Our monthly reports highlight how our suggested changes to sustainable substitutes and alternatives have decreased company expenses and helped the company gain positive recognition.

About Us
